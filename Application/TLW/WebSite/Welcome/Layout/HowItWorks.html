How It Works

Tri Living Well services include consulting with companies on their wellness program design, assessment reviews, incentive programs, and strategies on offering wellness as a benefit.

We offer a leading web platform for 24-hour access to programs, information, and health forums.  Tri Living Well provides health assessment tools to identify a user’s current health status, and provide customized programs that allow a user to begin a path to wellness, regardless of their current health.  Tri Living Well has designed a full spectrum of programs and services that allow all users to participate.

 Tri Living Well’s approach focuses around Fitness, Nutrition, and Wellness.
   
   

Tri Living Well’s approach focuses around Fitness, Nutrition, and Wellness.

Our Fitness programs are designed for any level of user. We offer multiple avenues for individuals to participate in exercise training regimens. Participants can opt for physical activity programs that are based in the gym, at home or in the great outdoors.

Our Nutrition programs are designed to provide a practical yet simple way to make healthy changes to your eating habits.  Instead of calorie deprivation or food restriction, we approach nutrition as a lifestyle change, designed to become a long-term habit.

Our Wellness approach is to focus on and measure what research shows as the major, modifiable lifestyle behavioral factors linked to chronic disease – physical activity, nutrition and tobacco use. We provide an actionable plan that measures change for any participant or within a company organization.
