About Us
Tri Living Well provides wellness solutions to companies designed for employees to Improve, Track, and Sustain their overall health, fitness and nutrition through our lifestyle management programs.  These programs are designed for individuals who are getting started with a healthier lifestyle, who want to be challenged and those who simply want to make healthy living a habit.

Our programs are designed to increase an individual’s overall level of health, which results in increased personal and professional productivity. Tri Living Well benefits employers by offering a wide range of employee benefits, performance and participation analysis, incentives strategies, aimed at reducing overall health care costs.

Our mission is simple: To provide leading wellness programs and services designed to make users healthier and more productive; and to help curb increasing healthcare utilization for employers.
Tri Living Well provides wellness programs and services to companies, self-insured health plans, and insurance brokerage firms.  Our services include consulting with companies on their wellness program design, assessment reviews, incentive programs, and strategies on offering wellness as a benefit.

We offer a leading web platform for 24-hour access to programs, information, and health forums.  Tri Living Well provides health assessment tools to identify a user’s current health status, and provide customized programs that allow a user to begin a path to wellness, regardless of their current health.  Tri Living Well has designed a full spectrum of programs and services that allow all users to participate.

Our approach provides a roadmap to success with its combination of fitness, nutrition, and wellness. We provide resources that allow users to establish long-term nutritional and exercise objectives, and tools to track and record food intake, activity level and health outlook.   Our activity forums, monitored by health coaches, provide guidance and motivation for users to achieve a healthier lifestyle.
